🎯 What You Can Report

  • Waste management issues
  • Road and infrastructure damage
  • Environmental violations
  • Public safety concerns
  • Water and sanitation problems
  • Street lighting issues

⏱️ What Happens Next?

  1. Your report is submitted instantly
  2. LGU receives and reviews your report
  3. Report is prioritized and assigned
  4. Action is taken to resolve the issue
  5. Status is updated in the system

💡 Tips for Better Reports

  • Be specific about the location
  • Provide detailed descriptions
  • Use the map to pinpoint locations
  • Include your contact information
  • Select the correct category
